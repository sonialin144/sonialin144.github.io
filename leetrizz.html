LeetRizz

An cute AI girlfriend that encourages you to do your LeetCode problems.

MHacks 2024

Devpost

Leetcoding is a crucial part of success in landing a job, yet many of us struggle to keep up with our Leetcode streak. We wanted to combine practical Leetcode recommendations with a playful twist, introducing an AI girlfriend that cheers you on and keeps you accountable. It's a blend of productivity and fun, designed to help users stay on track with their coding practice while receiving personalized encouragement.
