overview

Futour enlivens public spaces with geolocation and AR activations for passersby to enjoy and businesses to passively gain increased foot traffic. FUTOUR Playgrounds are specific public spaces that Urban Electronics have selected to overlay with Digital AR Content for anyone and everyone to engage with. At Playgrounds users can play mini games for rewards offered by FUTOUR or select Sponsors.
